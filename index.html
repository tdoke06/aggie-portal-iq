Scoring Methodology

Each prospect is evaluated on four independent factors, weighted and combined into a Fit Score from 0–100.

35%

Statistical Performance

2025 season statistics normalized within each position group. Position-specific key metrics (Passer Rating for QBs, Sacks + TFL for edge rushers, INT + PD for corners) are weighted and min-max scaled to a 0–100 score.

25%

Physical Profile

Height and weight evaluated against Texas A&M's positional ideal ranges informed by Mike Elko's defensive scheme. Players within the ideal window score 100; deviations are penalized proportionally.

25%

Scheme Fit

Combines position group need urgency (Critical / High / Medium / Low) with physical profile alignment to A&M's system. Critical positions receive a higher baseline score.

15%

Culture & Team Fit

Weighs eligibility remaining (freshmen score highest for program investment value) and transfer school pedigree. Power 4 transfers bring proven competition experience.
